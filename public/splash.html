Often, the most memorable moments of an adventure aren't captured on camera; they are made up of all the little things. We at Haiker know that you want to share more than the photos - you want to share the experience: the homely little inn you stayed at, the walk you went on off the beaten path before the sun rose are often the memories that define your trip. Haiker's here to help you.
